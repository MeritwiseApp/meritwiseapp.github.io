Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Godwin Agbo, the founder and CEO of Meritwise. I built this platform to solve a problem I experienced firsthand, the frustration of managing multiple bill payments across different platforms.

What started as a simple idea has grown into a trusted payment platform serving thousands of users across Nigeria.

My Journey

From frustration to innovation, how Meritwise came to life

The Problem

Like many Nigerians, I found myself juggling multiple apps and platforms just to pay basic bills – airtime, electricity, cable TV subscriptions. Each platform required separate logins, different payment methods, and the experience was often frustrating. I knew there had to be a better way.

The Vision

I envisioned a single platform that would make bill payments effortless. A place where you could handle all your transactions in one app, with a smooth user experience and rewards for your loyalty. That vision became Meritwise – your all-in-one premium payment platform.

Today & Beyond

Today, Meritwise serves thousands of users across Nigeria, processing millions in transactions monthly. But we're just getting started. Our mission is to become the leading payment platform in Africa, continuously innovating to make financial transactions simpler, faster, and more rewarding for everyone.

Our Mission & Vision

What drives us and where we're headed

To simplify financial transactions for millions of Africans by providing a seamless, secure, and rewarding payment platform that brings all essential services into one place. We're committed to making bill payments effortless while giving back to our users through cashback rewards and exceptional service.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted and innovative payment platform, empowering individuals and businesses to manage their finances with confidence. We envision a future where every transaction is fast, secure, and rewarding – a future where Meritwise is the go-to solution for all payment needs across the continent.
