1 Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our services to you:

1.1 Personal Information

Information you provide directly when you register or use our services:

  • Identity Information: Full name, date of birth, gender, nationality
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, residential address
  • Verification Documents: Government-issued ID (NIN, Driver's License, Passport, Voter's Card), BVN, selfie photographs
  • Financial Information: Bank account details, card information, transaction history, wallet balance
  • Account Credentials: Username, password (encrypted), security questions

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Information collected automatically when you use our platform:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
  • Usage Data: App features accessed, time spent, interaction patterns, preferences
  • Location Data: IP address, approximate geographic location (with your permission)
  • Transaction Data: Transaction amounts, dates, merchant information, payment methods used
  • Log Data: Access times, error logs, system activity

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Identity verification services (BVN validation, NIN verification)
  • Payment processors and financial institutions
  • Fraud prevention and security services
  • Social media platforms (if you choose to connect them)
  • Service providers for bills and utilities

Transparency: We only collect information necessary to provide our services, comply with legal requirements, and improve user experience.

4 Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

4.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: Industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Data Storage: Strong encryption for data at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based access controls and enhanced authentication mechanisms
  • Network Security: Firewalls, intrusion detection systems, DDoS protection
  • Secure Infrastructure: Enterprise-grade cloud hosting with industry-standard security
  • Password Protection: Bcrypt hashing for password storage

4.2 Organizational Safeguards

  • Regular security assessments and monitoring
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy
  • Strict internal access controls and security practices
  • Incident response and disaster recovery plans
  • Data breach notification procedures
  • Regular backup and redundancy systems

4.3 Your Security Role

You also play a crucial role in protecting your information:

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Never share your password, PIN, or OTP
  • Log out after using shared devices
  • Report suspicious activities immediately
  • Keep your app updated to the latest version

Data Breach: In the unlikely event of a security breach affecting your data, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities in accordance with applicable laws.

5 Your Privacy Rights

Under Nigerian Data Protection Regulation (NDPR), you have the following rights:

Your Data Rights
  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Opt out of certain data processing activities
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent for data processing at any time
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with data protection authorities

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, you can:

  • Access settings in the Meritwise app
  • Email us at support@meritwise.app
  • Contact our Data Protection Officer
  • Submit a written request to our office address

We will respond to your request within 30 days.

No Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

6 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and collect analytics data:

6.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic platform functionality, authentication, and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our services
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect data on usage patterns and performance metrics

6.2 Third-Party Analytics

We use analytics services to improve our app performance and user experience.

6.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings (block, delete, or limit cookies)
  • App settings (disable analytics tracking)
  • Device settings (limit ad tracking, reset advertising ID)

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

Transparency: We provide clear opt-in options for non-essential cookies and tracking.
